## Common mistakes to avoid

- **Don't tag every Anthropic employee in the main tweet.** That's spam-flag behavior. Tag the org account; mention people in replies if relevant.
- **Don't use 4+ hashtags.** Use 0–1. AI Twitter doesn't reward hashtags and they look amateur.
- **Don't post if your CI badge is red.** People click through; broken CI = instant credibility hit.
- **Don't ask for a retweet.** Explicit asks tank reply rates. Let the content earn it.
